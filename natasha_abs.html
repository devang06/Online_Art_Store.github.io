ABSTRACT ART BY NATASHA KHURRANA

Art Description:
Abstract art gives you the freedom to explore the artwork and assign your own meaning to the piece. This intensely personal process enriches a viewer's experience of an artwork. Understanding abstract art does not come naturally for everyone.

Art Description:
Abstract art is art that does not attempt to represent an accurate depiction of a visual reality but instead use shapes, colours, forms and gestural marks to achieve its effect. This painting will go with any mordern furniture in your home adding an extra vagant touch to it.

Art Description:
Abstract has meaning outside of art and so it is a unique form of art where in the resulting image is a kind of outline or summary of the process of making the image, whether expressive, emotive, media centric, etc. In this way, ghe image is representative of how the image is produced.
