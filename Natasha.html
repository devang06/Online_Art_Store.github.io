About:

A self taught artist since the beginning, I have always had interest and have been passionate about art since my childhood. Born and brought up in Delhi, I have always had interest and and felt immense happiness while practicing painting on the walls of my house. Developing my art skills over the years, I now feel that I am ready to showcase my talent into this beautiful world out there. Thus, here I am!
