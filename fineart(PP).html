FINE ART BY PRABHAKAR PACHPUTE

Art Description:
A very uniquely composed picture of a boat in primary colours against the white sand with a deep blue sky and random white clouds.
The absence of any human being makes it more inviting.

Art Description:
The reflections of the sun make the bay shine. Yellow to yellow, just like Van Gogh's sunflowers. The background perspective and the scenic image are absolutely mesmerizing.

Art Description:
