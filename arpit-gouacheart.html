GOUACHE ART BY ARPIT KOHLI

Art Description:
This original artwork was painted using Windsor & Newton gouache paints on Fabriano 300gsm watercolour paper The artwork comes safely packaged in an acid-free clear plastic sleeve and is shipped flat in a rigid mailer with a certificate of authenticity.

Art Description:
The Australian floral arrangement was inspired by Christmas and the quirky banksia and wattle plants. The artwork is sold unframed. And this painting is a beautiful display of vivid colors and immense beauty.

Art Description:
This painting is a gouache portrait from a contemporary painter that works primarily in aqueous media. In the process, we will learn about artistic anatomical structure, additional formal elements of image making, application of the paint and the materiality of the medium and a variety of surfaces gouache can be applied to.
